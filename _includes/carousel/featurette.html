What is Monochrome Collective? And what is going on?

Monochrome Collective is an initiative from several international fashion designers to promote young talent on the fashion industry in London. To achieve this, londoners will have a unique opportinity to explore new designs and garments away from the overcrowded and expensive London catwalks: The Monochrome Collective Pop Up Store and The Monochrome Collective Event.
