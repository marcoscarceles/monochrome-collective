What is Monochrome Collective? And what is going on?

Monochrome Collective is an initiative from several international fashion designers to promote young talent on the fashion industry in London. To achieve this, londoners will have a unique opportinity to explore new designs and garments away from the overcrowded and expensive London catwalks: The Monochrome Collective Pop Up Store and The Monochrome Collective Launch Event.

The Event. The best way to live and breath fashion.

On the Openning of the Pop Up Store, the Monochrome Collective will launch the The Monochrome Collective Launch Event. Come by to meet designers, photographers, models and other members of the industry and experience one of the most exiting events of the year. Get lost among the garments, sketchbooks and patterns while enjoying the music of our DJ. Plus... if you come wearing Monochrome, you get a complimentary welcome drink.
